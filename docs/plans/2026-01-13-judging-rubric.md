# Catholic Game Jam - Judging Rubric

## Award Categories

| Award | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Best Overall** | Highest combined score across all criteria. The complete package. |
| **Best Catholic Integration** | Highest score in faith element. The game that most meaningfully weaves Catholicism into gameplay. |
| **Best Gameplay** | Most fun to play, regardless of other factors. |
| **Honorable Mention** | Judge's discretionary pick for something special (best first-timer, most ambitious, unexpected gem, etc.) |

## Scoring Criteria (1-5 Scale)

### Catholic Integration

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 5 | Faith is central to gameplay mechanics, not just theming. Couldn't work without it. |
| 4 | Strong integration - Catholic elements shape the experience meaningfully |
| 3 | Solid Catholic theme, but could swap to another setting without major changes |
| 2 | Surface-level - crosses/saints present but feel decorative |
| 1 | Barely present or feels forced/disconnected |

### Gameplay/Fun

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 5 | Genuinely fun, would play again. Clear goals, satisfying loop. |
| 4 | Enjoyable experience with minor friction |
| 3 | Functional and playable, but not especially memorable |
| 2 | Confusing or frustrating, but has moments |
| 1 | Broken or unplayable |

### Polish/Completeness

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 5 | Feels finished. No major bugs, clear instructions, polished presentation. |
| 4 | Complete experience with minor rough edges |
| 3 | Playable from start to finish, some obvious gaps |
| 2 | Core is there but clearly unfinished |
| 1 | Prototype/proof of concept only |

## How Winners Are Determined

### Best Overall
- Add up scores from all three criteria (max 15 points)
- Highest total wins
- Ties broken by Catholic Integration score (since that's the jam's core theme)

### Best Catholic Integration
- Highest score in that single criterion
- Ties broken by combined total

### Best Gameplay
- Highest score in that single criterion
- Ties broken by combined total

### Honorable Mention
- No formula - judges discuss and pick something that stood out
- Could recognize: best first-timer, most creative concept, best art despite low polish, etc.

## Feedback

Every submission gets brief written feedback (2-3 sentences) highlighting strengths and one growth area.
